# 🇮🇳 Indian Tourism Analysis 2025 (SQL Project)

## 📌 Objective

Analyze tourism data across Indian states to identify patterns in tourist visits, revenue generation, and seasonal trends.

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

* SQL (MySQL)

---

## 📊 Dataset Overview

* Records: 432
* Coverage: 36 States & Union Territories
* Period: January – December 2025

---

## 🔍 SQL Concepts Applied

* Aggregations (SUM, AVG, COUNT)
* Window Functions (RANK, ROW_NUMBER, LAG)
* Joins (INNER, LEFT, RIGHT)
* Subqueries & CASE WHEN
* Indexing

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Delhi has the highest number of tourists (42.86M)
* Maharashtra leads in foreign tourists (1.57M)
* Business travel generates the highest revenue (₹4,494 Cr)
* Domestic tourists outnumber foreign tourists (25:1 ratio)
* November is the peak tourism month (111.8M tourists)

---

## 💡 Business Questions Answered

* Which states generate the highest tourism revenue?
* Which month has peak tourist activity?
* What is the domestic vs foreign tourist contribution?
* Which purpose of visit generates maximum revenue?
